La Lechuza

This is a Visual Novel that I worked on over a 2 week period for a class project. It adapts the story of La Lechuza, which is rooted in Mexican and Texano folklore. According to legend, La Lechuza was a witch who made a deal with the devil and could transform into a giant owl. I designed the characters and lead the coding part of the project.

TeamFight!

TeamFight! is a physical card game I made over a 5 week period for a final class project. I wanted to replicate Dungeons and Dragon's style of combat that you can play against your friends and without the need of a long D&D campaign. The characters are based on common classes you might see in RPG fantasy games like D&D, Dark Souls or Diablo.

Strawberry Festival

The setting and characters are based on Stardew Valley, including what gifts the characters like. I completed this project over a 2 week period for a class project using Inky. Ink is a narrative scripting language, and I used variables, loops, and conditions to make the game more interactive and less linear. I also used "callbacks" in the dialouge to make it feel like your choices matter.

Lotería: US Army Edition

Lotería is a traditional Mexican game similar to Bingo and is full of references to different aspects of Mexican culture. When I was younger, I played using beans as markers. This was my way of bringing together two significant parts of my life. This was a completely personal project and I did all of the art myself in one afternoon.

Original Character Concept

This is an orginal character I created. The inspiration for this character came from a few different places, including Kill Bill, Yakuza, League of Legends, Bleach, and Chainsaw Man. The first image is the fully-colored, digital sketch I did using Procreate on my iPad. The front and side profiles are the references I used to create a 3D model in Blender. Next is the 3D model with seams and UV unwrap. And finally, the completed 3D model doing a run cycle. This entire project was the culminating assignment for a class done over a 10 week term.

The Witch is Dead - Companion

The Witch is Dead is a one page RPG that I listened to from an episode of my favorite D&D podcast, Dungeons and Daddies. I really liked the game and the idea behind it, so I thought I'd give it a try for myself. Then I realized, I could probably build a companion tool/app to go along with it so I could play with my friends over discord. I made the app over one weekend using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's pretty simple, but still I am pretty proud of it.

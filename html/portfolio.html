Portfolio

TeamFight!

TeamFight! is a physical card game I made for a class project. I wanted to replicate Dungeons and Dragon's style of combat that you can play against your friends and without the need of a long D&D campaign. The characters are based on common classes you might see in RPG fantasy games like D&D, Dark Souls or Diablo.

Lotería: US Army Edition

Lotería is a traditional Mexican game similar to Bingo and is full of references to different aspects of Mexican culture. When I was younger, I played using beans as markers. This was my way of bringing together two significant parts of my life.

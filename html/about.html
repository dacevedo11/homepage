About

Hello, my name is Daniel! I am an undergraduate student at UC Santa Cruz and a research assistant at the Game User Interaction and Intelligence Lab. I will be graduating in June 2024 with a Bachelor's degree in Art & Design: Games and Playable Media.

As you might guess, I love video games! My favorites include The Legend of Zelda, Pok√©mon, Dragon Quest, Fire Emblem, and League of Legends. I'm also a big fan of tabletop games, especially Dungeons & Dragons! Also, I love anime! Some of my favorites are One Piece, Attack on Titan, Steins;Gate, and Bleach. My other hobbies include sports, drawing, reading, and movies!

Before I was a college student, I served in the US Army for 4 years as a combat medic and paratrooper. During my time at UCSC, I have worked towards creating a meaningful community for veterans and military-connected students to help them achieve their academic goals and shatter negative stereotypes. I am also president of the UCSC Student Veteran Association and volunteer with Vets 4 Vets Santa Cruz.

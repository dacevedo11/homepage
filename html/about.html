About Me

Hello, my name is Daniel! I am an undergraduate student at UC Santa Cruz and a research assistant at the Game User Interaction and Intelligence Lab. I will be graduating in June 2024 with a Bachelor's degree in Art & Design: Games and Playable Media.

Before I was a college student, I served in the US Army for 4 years as a combat medic and paratrooper. As a student veteran, I know firsthand the struggles that veterans face while navigating the educational system. During my time at UCSC, I have worked towards creating a meaningful community for veterans and military-connected students to help them achieve their academic goals and shatter negative stereotypes. I am also president of the UCSC Student Veteran Association.

Games are my passion! Some of my most cherished memories involve video games, and they are undoubtedly a huge part of my life. I learned early on that games offer so much more than just entertainment. Thererfore, my goal is to be accepted into a graduate program where I can reseach the applications of games and interactive media, and that will ultimately prepare me to work in the gaming industry.
